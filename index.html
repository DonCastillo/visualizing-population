About this project

This site intends to educate people about the recent trends in population as it hit eight billion in 2022.

People are visual learners. On this page, the use of different typographic compositions, font faces, font weights, and icons made communicating facts and statistics about population a lot better than just reading a long block of text or tabular data. The use of CSS animations helped draw the attention of the readers to continue reading the text.
